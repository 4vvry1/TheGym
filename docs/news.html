Milan Sets the Stage

On September 7, 2025, the city of Milan, Italy, became the center of the bodybuilding world as it proudly hosted the IFBB ProMuscle Italy Pro, a highly anticipated qualifier for the 2025 Mr. Olympia. Organized by promoter Barbara Bagnuolo, this prestigious two-day event (September 6–7) brought together elite athletes from across the globe, all striving for the opportunity to showcase their physiques on bodybuilding’s biggest stage.

The competition featured three major divisions—Men’s Open Bodybuilding, Women’s Bikini, and Women’s Wellness—each filled with remarkable talent and fierce determination. With the Olympia qualification on the line, every competitor pushed their limits, resulting in one of the most exciting and closely watched contests of the 2025 IFBB Pro League season.
